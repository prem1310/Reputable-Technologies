Our Products

Comprehensive software solutions designed to streamline your business operations

EzAttendancePro

EzAttendancePro Software makes maintaining the attendance and leaves data easy, simple and faster by incorporating dynamic masters based on the company’s organizational structure; defines various groups to govern late coming, week off, overtime policies, compensatory off policies, paid holidays and so on.

EzVMS

EzVMS (Visitor Management System) is a software solution or platform designed to track and manage visitors entering and exiting a facility or organization. It streamlines the process of visitor registration, check-in, and check-out, enhancing security, efficiency, and overall visitor experience.

Learn More

EzPayrollPro

EzPayroll (Payroll Management System) is a software application or system designed to automate and streamline the process of managing an organization's payroll. It is used by businesses of all sizes to accurately calculate and process employee salaries, wages, deductions, and other related tasks.

Learn More

EzAccessPro

Access Control Software is a comprehensive security solution designed for modern organizations, integrating mobile access, QR codes, facial recognition, fingerprint scanning, and card-based authentication.It enhances security, streamlines operations, and provides a seamless user experience for corporate offices, societies, educational institutions, and high-security facilities.

Learn More

EzCRM

EzCRM (Customer Relationship Management System) is a software tool or platform that businesses use to manage and analyze their interactions with current and potential customers. CRM systems help organizations streamline their sales, marketing, and customer service processes, enabling them to build and maintain strong customer relationships.

Learn More

EzCanteen

EzCanteen (Canteen Management System) is a software application or platform designed to streamline and automate various operations related to managing a canteen or cafeteria. It is commonly used in schools, universities, corporate offices, and other institutions where there is a need to efficiently manage food services for a large number of people. The system typically integrates multiple functions, including menu planning, ordering, inventory management, payment processing, and reporting.
