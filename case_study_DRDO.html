DRDO's Research Center Imarat

Customized ERP Software for Defense Research Excellence

DRDO's Research Center Imarat (RCI), a premier defense research laboratory in Hyderabad, transformed their operations through advanced ERP solutions with enhanced security measures and seamless integration capabilities.

Project Overview

DRDO's Research Center Imarat (RCI) is a premier defense research laboratory located in Hyderabad, India. RCI is involved in the development of advanced defense technologies and systems. To streamline their operations and enhance security measures, RCI required a customized ERP software solution.

The key requirements included improved security measures, labor registration management, visitor management system, integration with existing in-house ERP, and generating comprehensive MIS reports. Additionally, integration with Rosslare Hardware's was necessary to ensure seamless access control and key management.
